Raspberry Hand Pies

Ingredients

  • 1 package frozen puff pastry, thawed according to package
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablesoons cornstarch
  • 12 ounces raspberries, washed
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 large egg

Tools Needed

  • Baking Sheet
  • Parchment Paper
  • Oven
  • 1 small bowl
  • 1 large bowl
  • measuring cups
  • tablespoons and teaspoons
  • fork
  • spatula

Step 1

Preheat oven to 400ºF

Step 2

Line a baking sheet with parchment paper

Step 3

Unfold puff pastry sheet and cut out 4 squares

Step 4

Place pastry squares onto the prepared baking sheet

Step 5

Crack 1 egg into small bowl, whisk thoroughly with a fork, and set aside egg mixture

Step 6

Place 1/3 cup granulated sugar into a large bowl

Step 7

Add 2 tablespoons of cornstarch to the sugar and stir with spatula to combine

Step 8

Add 12 ounces of raspberries and 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice to sugar/cornstarch mixture and mix ingredients

Step 9

Spoon raspberry filling into one half of each of the pastry square

Step 10

Brush edges of pastry squares with egg mixture

Step 11

Fold dough over raspberry filling and crimp edges with a fork to seal

Step 12

Cut 3 small vents on top of each hand pie

Step 13

Sprinkle the tops of the pies with sugar

Step 14

Bake until golden, around 20-25 minutes

Step 15

Let cool for around 10 minutes. Enjoy!
